5 ## 1 Mathematics and Algorithms
6 
7 A Crank-Nicolson semi-implicit finite difference scheme is used to obtain concentration of lithium in the sphere, c(iapp, r), at each time step by approximating the partial differential equation (PDE) solution.
8 
9 This is accurate to second order both spatially and temporally, i.e. O(∆r2) & O(∆t2).
10 
11 SPAMS models the charging and discharging of a lithium ion battery using a Crank-Nicolson semi-implicit finite difference scheme to obtain the
12 concentration of lithium in a sphere, **c**(i<sub>app</sub>, **r**), at each time step, and has the following features:
13 * Apply a constant, stepwise or custom current
14 * Options for parallelism
15 * Extend the model to a full battery
16 * First order sensitivty analysis
