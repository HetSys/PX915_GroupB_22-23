1 # SPAMS - Single PArticle Model Solver
2 
3 A half single particle model by Group B for the 2023 PX915 group project.
4 
5 ## Program Features
6 
7 SPAMS models the charging and discharging of a lithium ion battery using a Crank-Nicolson semi-implicit finite difference scheme to obtain the
8 concentration of lithium in a sphere, **c**(i<sub>app</sub>, **r**), at each time step, and has the following features:
9 * Apply a constant, stepwise or custom current
10 * Options for parallelism
11 * Extend the model to a full battery
12 * First order sensitivty analysis
