A half single particle model by Group B for the 2023 PX915 group project.\hypertarget{md__home_chem_msuvnj__p_x915__group_b_22_23__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md1}{}\doxysection{Program Features}\label{md__home_chem_msuvnj__p_x915__group_b_22_23__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md1}
SPAMS models the charging and discharging of a lithium ion battery using a Crank-\/\+Nicolson semi-\/implicit finite difference scheme to obtain the concentration of lithium in a sphere, {\bfseries{c}}(i\textsubscript{app}, {\bfseries{r}}), at each time step, and has the following features\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Apply a constant, stepwise or custom current
\item Options for parallelism
\item Extend the model to a full battery
\item First order sensitivty analysis
\end{DoxyItemize}\hypertarget{md__home_chem_msuvnj__p_x915__group_b_22_23__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md2}{}\doxysection{Dependencies}\label{md__home_chem_msuvnj__p_x915__group_b_22_23__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md2}
